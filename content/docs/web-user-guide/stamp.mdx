---
title: Stamp
description: This section explains how to stamp files using the Integritas Web App
---

To learn more about how stamping works and its role in ensuring data integrity,
check our [technical documentation on stamping](/docs/technical-docs/stamping).

### 1. Navigate to the Upload Data Page

Once inside the Integritas Web App, go to the **Upload Data** page to upload and stamp your data.

### 2. Upload File(s)

Select files manually or **drag and drop** them into the upload box.

### 3. Initiate Upload

Click **Upload** to stamp and upload your data.
