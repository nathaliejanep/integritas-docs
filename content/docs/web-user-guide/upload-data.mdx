---
title: Upload Data
description: This section explains how to upload and stamp files using the Integritas Web App
---

### 1. Navigate to the Upload Data Page

Once inside the Integritas Web App, go to the **Upload Data** page to upload and stamp your data.

### 2. Upload File(s)

Select files manually or **drag and drop** them into the upload box.

  title='Integritas Web App - stamp upload box'
  type='warning'
  className='w-[400px] overflow-hidden'
>
  <ImageZoom
    width={400}
    height={400}
    alt='Integritas Web App - stamp upload box'
    src='/guide-dapp/upload.png'
  />
</Callout>
### 3. Initiate Upload

Click **Upload** to stamp and upload your data.
