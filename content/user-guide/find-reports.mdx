## Find Reports

### 1. Navigate to My Uploads

Go to **My Uploads** to see all your uploaded files.

- **Search for files**  
   Use the search bar to quickly find a specific file.

- **Add file label**  
   Under the **File Label** column, click **Add Label** to tag a file for easier identification.

### 2. View Detailed Report

Click **Details** next to the report you want to view to access the full verification breakdown.

## Access Verified Reports

### Download PDF Report

In **My Uploads**, find the file you want and click the **Download** button in the **PDF** column to download your verification report.

### Access NFT Proof

In **My Uploads**, click **Details** for the file.  
Scroll to the **NFT Proof** section and click **Link to NFT** to view the blockchain record in the **Minima Block Explorer**.
